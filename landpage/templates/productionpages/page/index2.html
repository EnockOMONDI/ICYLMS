Why study with us?

The Leadership and Development Academy (LADA) is established in Africa as a joint initiative of Springdale College UK now known as ForMission College, accredited by York St John University and Newman University in Birmingham, Africa for SDGs and ICY AFRICA which runs the Springdale Foundation and other global partners. The LADA aims to build a new community of transformational leaders with practical application on Leadership, Governance and Development. We believe that when youths are capacitated with leadership and development skills, knowledge and attitude, then they become active citizens in their respective communities and countries..
