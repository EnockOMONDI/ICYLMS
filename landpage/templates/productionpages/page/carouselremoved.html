Leadership and Development Academy

The Leadership and Development Academy (LADA) is established in Africa as a joint initiative of Springdale College UK now known as ForMission College, accredited by York St John University and Newman University in Birmingham, Africa for SDGs and ICY AFRICA which runs the Springdale Foundation and other global partners.
