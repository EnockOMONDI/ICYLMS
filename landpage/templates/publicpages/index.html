Why US

The Leadership and Development Academy (LADA) is established in Africa as a joint initiative of Springdale College UK now known as ForMission College, accredited by York St John University and Newman University in Birmingham, Africa for SDGs and ICY AFRICA which runs the Springdale Foundation and other global partners. We believe that when youths are capacitated with leadership and development skills, knowledge and attitude, then they become active citizens in their respective communities and countries.



Quick Facts: Online LADA

  • Taught in Multiple Language
  • Delivered online
  • Uses the unique Action Learning methodology
  • Focus 100% on real-time topics of relevance to leadership and Development challenges facing Africa
  • Engage with a network of alumni and leaders
  • Come up with a practical prototype aligned to a specific sustainable development goal(SDGs) to be implemented at the community level
  • Earn a Certificate upon completion of study

Study with us? Africa’s future leaders

The LADA courses are designed to expose Junior African leaders to the complexities of Leadership, Governance and development issues facing the African continent with the aim of equipping them with the knowledge, skills and attitude necessary for transformative leadership and sustainable development. Each learner will be expected as an individual or a group of fellows to come up with an innovative, implementable and scalable idea that meets the social, economic, environmental and climate challenges facing Africa.
